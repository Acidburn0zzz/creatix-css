Imixs Workflow makes your Java Business Applications flexible!

Imixs Workflow is a BPM Framework for building Business Applications. Imixs Workflow provides your with a transactional and scalable workflow engine and an easy to use modelling tool. Imixs Workflow allows you to design and run any kind of business process on all platforms.

Use open source...

Imixs Workflow is free software. You can run the framework on any platform, and use it for any kind of business application. Imixs Workflow takes care of messaging, security, documentation and persistence of all your business data. Using the Eclipse based Imixs Modeler you can design and change your workflows at run-time.
Read how Imixs Workflow works...

... and use open standards

Imixs Workflow provides a full featured Workflow Management System (WFMS) based on the Java EE specification. It fulfills all requirements of a scalable, transactional, robust and simple deployable BPM Engine. Imixs Workflow enables you to build enterprise applications faster than ever before.

General Goals and License

Imixs Workflow provides the results of the project under the GPL with a dual-license model, to meet the usage and distribution requirements of different types of users.

If you are developing and distributing open source applications under the GPL License, then you are free to use Imixs Workflow under the GPL License. For OEMs, ISVs, and VARs who distribute Imixs Workflow with their products, and do not license and distribute their source code under the GPL, Imixs Workflow provides a flexible Commercial License.
